A little bit about myself.

Coming from a psychology background, I interned as a UX Researcher @GfK back in 2017, where I conducted many interviews and experienced how users encounter new interfaces and products on a daily basis. Still interested in the topic I started to work as a freelancer in UX Research. However, I figured that I want to continue creating interfaces as well as keep doing research. Therefore, I enrolled in the M.Sc. Human Computer Interaction at the University of Nottingham, UK where I learned about UX design methodologies, programming and data science.
In December 2019, I graduated from my master's program with distinction and kept educating myself further in UX Design, UX Research and Front-End Development. Since March 2020 I am working as a Product Designer for Kaufland, where we create business software for sellers to sell on our marketplace.
